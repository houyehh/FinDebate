# Alpha Gym Competition Video Script

## Scene 1: Alpha Gym
AI gives answers. Alpha Gym trains judgment. It is a local-first investment decision gym for practicing market calls before accepting a model's conclusion.

## Scene 2: The product is a training loop
Most finance tools jump straight to recommendations. Alpha Gym reverses that pattern: replay the market, make your own call, reveal evidence and outcomes, then review how your reasoning performed.

## Scene 3: Memorable product shell
The outer experience uses a luxurious decision-hall visual identity, while the workbench keeps conventional chart colors, dense market data, and practical controls for repeated use.

## Scene 4: Market Replay hides the future
Market Replay places the user at a past date and hides future prices until submission. Each drill shows price structure, technical indicators, fundamentals, news and theme context, and AI usage prompts.

## Scene 5: The workbench starts with the chart
The decision workbench starts with a large chart. Users can toggle moving averages, Bollinger Bands, volume, KD, and MACD, while a fixed inspector keeps daily values visible without blocking the chart.

## Scene 6: Evidence is split into readable grids
Below the chart, evidence is organized into compact grids. Fundamentals include valuation and quality signals such as P E, market cap, margins, profitability, debt, growth, and dividend context when data is available.

## Scene 7: AI becomes a new dimension
In OpenAI API mode, GPT powers AI summaries, bull and bear debate, judge scoring, and personalized coach feedback. If quota or model access fails, Alpha Gym clearly labels deterministic fallback content instead of pretending it came from GPT.

## Scene 8: Coaching is based on the user's answer
After each practice answer, the coach receives the user's side, confidence, rationale, evidence weights, correct side, visible evidence, and real result. The feedback names the likely blind spot and the next training focus.

## Scene 9: Live Desk turns today's ticker into a record
Live Desk uses the same workbench for today's market data. A saved decision moves into Portfolio Lab with ticker, entry price, timestamp, side, confidence, rationale, and AI agreement.

## Scene 10: Portfolio and Review close the loop
Portfolio Lab tracks current decisions and manual entries. Review Center separates practice answer records from live judgment records, so users can edit, delete, and revisit the right context.

## Scene 11: Built with Codex and GPT-5.6
Codex was the engineering partner for the FastAPI backend, React interface, SQLite schema, yfinance data layer, tests, and video pipeline. GPT five point six powers the intended AI path through structured JSON validated by the backend.

## Scene 12: Alpha Gym
Alpha Gym is for people learning to think with AI before trusting AI. The project is on GitHub at houyehh slash FinDebate.
